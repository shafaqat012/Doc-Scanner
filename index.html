Privacy Policy

App Name: Doc Scanner

Package: com.shafaqattech.docscanner

Developer: Shafaqat Tech

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

1. Overview

Doc Scanner is a mobile application that allows users to scan physical documents using their device camera, manage scanned images, and convert them to PDF. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties. All data generated by the app remains entirely on your device.

Data Accessed Locally:

  • Camera Images: Captured solely for the purpose of document scanning and processing.
  • Local Media Files: Accessed only when you explicitly choose to import an existing image from your device gallery.

3. Camera Permission

Doc Scanner requests access to your device camera solely to capture document images for scanning. Camera images are processed on-device and saved to your local app storage. No images are uploaded to any server or transmitted over the internet.

4. Storage and Media Permissions

To allow you to import existing images and save your generated PDFs, the app requests the following access based on your Android version:

  • Android 12 and below: Requests read/write access to external storage to import and export files.
  • Android 13 and above: Uses modern scoped media permissions to access only the specific images you select.

All files generated by the app are stored in the app's private directory or your device's local storage and are never uploaded anywhere.

5. Data Storage

  • All scanned documents, enhanced images, and generated PDFs are stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is stored on external servers, cloud services, or third-party databases.
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently unless you have manually exported files to a public folder on your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties. The app does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking libraries.

When you use the Share feature, your device's native sharing mechanism is used to send files (e.g., PDFs) to other applications of your choice. We have no control over how those third-party apps handle your files once shared.

7. Third-Party Services

Doc Scanner does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect personal information. The app functions entirely offline.

8. Children's Privacy

Doc Scanner does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not target children and contains no features that would require or encourage children to provide personal information.

9. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your scanned documents depends on your device's own security features (screen lock, file encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure and updated.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
